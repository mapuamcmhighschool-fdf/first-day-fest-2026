BEFORE THE BELL — First Day Fest 2026–2027

Open index.html in a modern browser.

Features:
- Personalized student check-in
- Persistent progress via localStorage
- Achievement badges and mission tracker
- Campus door exploration
- Mapúa MCM trivia
- Readiness checklist and personality result
- Grade 11 pathway explorer
- Club booth stickers
- Freedom Wall stored on the device

Notes:

- GSAP, Tailwind CSS, html2canvas, jsPDF, and Google Fonts are loaded through CDNs.
